ABOUT

  • Welcome to Speak Up With Us Canada! We are a Canadian organization striving to help youth gain the necessary skills needed for the workforce!
  • Speak up with us is a mentorship program that aims to bridge the gap between high school and the workforce
  • Lessons are provided on personal branding, scholarship applications, personal finance and more!

OUR MISSION

  • Provide youth mentorship and networking opportunities
  • Help develop financial literacy skills
  • Aid in University/College applications
  • Career development opportunities
